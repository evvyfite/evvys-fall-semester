Teacher Background

Enric is a renowned architect from Figueres, Spain. An alumna of the Barcelona School of Architecture, he has been part of several major architecture exhibitions around the world, with a focus on innovation and interdisciplinary work. He has held numerous teaching positions within the Architecture program and Honors College at Virginia Tech, and is often called upon to give lectures at numerous additional universities. He has also worked closely with various prestigious design companies such as the Italian lighting company iGuzzini.
