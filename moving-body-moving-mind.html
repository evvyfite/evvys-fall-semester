Teacher Background

Dr. Basso teaches classes in neuroscience, dance, and Human Nutrition, Foods and Exercise. She conducts research in Neuroscience, movement, dance, and behavioral science through Virginia Tech, and teaches classes that explore the connections between the body and the mind. She got her B.A. in Neuroscience and Dance, a CYT in Hatha Yoga from Vibhuti Yoga School, Inc., and her Ph.D. in Behavioral and Neural Science from Rutgers University. She is credited in several peer-reviewed articles on the connection between the body and the mind.
