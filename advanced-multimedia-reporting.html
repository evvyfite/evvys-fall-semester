Teacher Background

Jared is an experienced professor the in the School of Communication, teaching Journalism classes such as Visual Media, Photojournalism, Advanced Multimedia Reporting, and Digital Newsroom. He is a co-author on a publication about the role of the press in encouraging public strength during health crises, and has conducted various research in media and in journalism. He has several years of experience in the field of journalism, which he now applies to the classroom. He got his B.A. in Journalism from the University of Arkansas and his M.A. in Communication from the University of Colorado Denver.
